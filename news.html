BLM Statement & Fundraiser

The Brown Ultimate Frisbee community is outraged by the recent murders of Ahmaud Arbery, Breonna Taylor, Tony McDade, and George Floyd. We condemn the continued patterns of systemic violence and oppression toward Black people across the United States. This should not have to be said in 2020, but we support and stand with Black communities across the country and demand their liberation now. Simultaneously, we know that this moment calls not only for speaking out but also for looking inward. Over the coming weeks, months, and years, we will engage in deeper dialogue within and between our five teams, and commit to anti-racist actions that extend beyond our fundraiser.
