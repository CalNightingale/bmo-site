About Us

For over 40 years, the Brown Ultimate program has churned out elite players and coaches all across the country. Brown Ultimate, commonly referred to as “The Program,” claims three men’s national titles (2000, 2005, and 2019) and five Callahan winners. BMo returned to College Nationals in 2018 after a 10-year hiatus, thanks to the superior coaching of Sam Lehman and Jake Smart, whose commitment and acuity have taken BMo from kangaroo court to nationals contender.

News

CCC Recap: “BMo Takes the Crown in Athens, Georgia”

BMo won the Classic City Classic in Athens, GA this past weekend! Our trip to Georgia began at midday on Friday. BMo traveled in style in accordance with our Fancy Flight tradition. After touching down in the Peach State, the gate agent took our photo, and everyone headed off to Jason Tapper’s house.

Lobster Pot Recap: “A Rainy Mainey Weekend”

Brownian Motion made its glorious fall debut at last weekend’s Lobster Pot in Portland, Maine. We split into four cars and left Brown’s campus on a beautiful Friday afternoon. Excitement abounded. After much car bonding, we arrived at our tournament lodging: a quaint seaside Airbnb for most and Zach Merrill’s wonderful house for the elites. Everyone convened at the Airbnb to hear some inspirational words from the captains, and then to make our traditional spray-painted jerseys.

Nationals Recap: Cincin-natties

In case you missed it, we qualified for Nationals by winning the New England Region in thrilling fashion, just weeks ago. Since that glorious day, the Hellfish have achieved the pinnacle of student-athlete-dom as we balanced the grueling schedule of Training Week, finals, and NBA conference finals. We perfected our sets over the two practices per day and grew ever closer as teammates, knowing that this current iteration of BMo had a lifetime measured in days.
